Community Bulletin - July Edition

Food Festival - July 15
Our annual Food Festival returns to Central Park on July 15 from 10 AM to 6 PM! Featuring more than 30 local food vendors, live cooking demos, a kids’ food zone, and live music from community bands. Entry is free, and food tickets will be available on-site. Come hungry and ready for fun!

Community Cleanup - July 22
Join hands to keep our community clean and green. The cleanup drive begins at 8 AM sharp, starting from Town Hall and covering nearby parks and streets. Volunteers will receive gloves, bags, and refreshments. Certificates of participation will be given. Let’s work together for a better tomorrow!

Talent Show -July 29
Don't miss the grand Talent Show happening at the Community Center on July 29 at 5 PM. Enjoy performances from local singers, dancers, comedians, and more. It’s a celebration of creativity and spirit. Admission is free and open to all. Come support your neighbors and be inspired!

Bulletin Notes:
- Community Library will be closed on July 20 for maintenance.
